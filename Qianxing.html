Qianxing Public Welfare

This is a student-initiated, non-profit website. Our mission is to achieve the interconnection between charitable volunteer groups targeting at rural areas and to help people gain a better understanding of rural charity. On this website, users can initiate a new public welfare activity or join an activity that they are interested, moreover, users can record, publish their activity and leader can post important notices or share documents such as meeting records between team members.

We hope this website can encourage more and more people who have the ability to help people in rural areas to take actions.

THE DEMANDS
At the beginning, we circumscribed our target users. Target users mainly consist of following two parts: First the social groups who need help, second the volunteer organizations who want to help, including university associations such as Loving Heart Society of Peking University and enterprises.
Then, in order to find out the needs of target users we carried out several investigations including online surveys and interviews. We summarized those responses into three main points.
  • Huge demand of volunteering in rural areas.
  • City Volunteers want to know how to leave for and help the countryside.
  • Volunteers long for sharing experiences and recording their own feelings and thoughts.
3
THE VISION
Qianxing will be a social networking platform for charitable volunteers to form mutual connections in rural charity:
  • Charity updates posting: to share experiences with others.
  • Social networking interaction: to interact and communicate with others.
  • Interest-based choice: to choose the events that suit you best.
Qianxing will be an online learning platform for charitable volunteers to learn more about rural charity:
  • Presenting rural demands: to provide the charitable groups with information.
  • Involvement in editing: to compile the encyclopedia of rural charity demand.
  • Open online courses: to equip users with knowledge with regard to charity.
Considering the time we were able to put in, we decide to fulfill the first vision, social networking platform, in our first website version. After that, we will add more features to the website and make it an online learning platform.

OUR WEBSITE
(1) Homepage
This is a web page that gives visitors the first impression about what we do and what they can do with our website. This page is responsible for showing visitors a simple guideline about how to use our website and more important, display latest information of welfare activities.
Homepage 1.0
Homepage 2.0
The main difference between Homepage 1.0 and Homepage 2.0 is the present of activities. In Homepage 2.0, I adopt "waterfall" layout for the following reasons.
First, visitors are more likely to be attracted to images and "waterfall" layout can provide space for more pictures. Second, this kind of layout enable visitors to gain information more effective and also it is easy for them to view more information by simply scrolling down. Third, "waterfall" make all the activities' information well-spaced and elegant, and by simpify the information of activity and only highlight those information needed, visitors may focus more on the content of a certain activity instead of secondary Info.
(2) Activity Page
Activity Page is the web page that present details of a welfare activity, including basic information of an activity like time, place, recruit deadline, notice and team members. Also, it contains articles and short texts that are relevant to the activity. Thus, visitors can browse this page to get to know about an activity and team members are able to manage their documents and share stories on this web page.
Activity Page 1.0
Activity Page 2.0
After several debates about the Activity Page 1.0, we decide to add a new function to this page, that is, classification of articles and short texts. We reached a consensus that each team has different needs, for example, a team needs to have a special space to store meeting records while the other wish to place their dairies together, so it would be more reasonable for them to manage their own files of their activities.
Thus, in Activity Page 2.0, I took the new function into account and redesign the page. First, I rearranged the layout of team cover, information and team members. I also add a space for pictures and latest Info, thus, readers can view most of information at the first glance without scrolling down. Second, I divided the page into two part to present articles, short texts, milestone and so on. Articles and short texts took most of the space since they are more important and they can be classified into different groups such as resource center, meeting records and team journal.
(3) Social Page
This is a web page that provide a platform for all kinds of users to communicate. Users can post articles or short texts and photos that concern with different public welfare activities on this page, they can also make comments or reply under other users' articles or short texts.
Social Page 1.0
Social Page 2.0
The main improvement I made of this web page is change of color and layout of username.
First, I made the "plus" button above the input box invisible and by simply clicking the text, user is able to add another activity since the function is not commonly used ( normally users will post a article or short text to only one activity ). Second, I changed the color of "sent" button and make it more eay-catching to encourage users to post their own articles. Third, I changed the color of icon of "thanks", "comments" and "forwards" to make the overall style more consistent. Fourth, I changed the position of username from the bottom of user picture to the right of it not only to make username easier to identify, but make it in conformity with most of users' habit these days.
