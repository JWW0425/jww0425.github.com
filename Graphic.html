Graphic Design

Design to me is like a game, it is so subtle that by simply changing a color, one is able to make a huge difference to the whole picture. I was the director of the publicity department of CAPU (Cycling Association of Peking University), and our department was in charge of designing promotional materials such as posters, flyers and T-shirts. I learned Photoshop on my own and I'm amazed by how flexible this tool can be.

I have designed several things, some for the publicity department and some just for fun. I am eager to learn more, about the wonders of color effects and the fantastic layout of elements.
