Quantitative strategies for everyone.

GPX Trade

This is a website for quantitative investing. Typically, quantitative investing is implemented by people who have spent time in the physics, math, computer science, or statistics disciplines. What GPX does is to provide everyone equal chance to do quantitative investing even he or she has no experience in math or computer science. By simply putting in basic information to generate a strategy or by buying strategy in the strategy pool, user can obtain the proper strategy, moreover, user can sell those strategies which had good performance.

I joined GPX Trade in September, 2015 as designer. I worked together with the team to improve and perfect the website.

THE OPPORTUNITIES
Quantitative investment is not a new concept, it has developed for 30 years abroad and has been acknowledged by more and more investors. However, it is still rare to see individual investor adopting quantitative strategy in China, actually, some of the investors are new to this concept. This is because most of the investors view quantitative investment as something incomprehensible and are daunted by the convoluted formulas and indexes.
Yet with the good performance of quantitative investment these days, investors in China gradually realized the advantage of quantitative strategy. Also, since this is a new field in China, there will be a lot of opportunities and less competition.
2
THE VISION
We want to make quantitative strategy available for every individual investors rather than limited to only institutional investors. Users can generate their own quantitative strategy on our website by putting in basic information, or, can buy or sell strategy using our website.
Quantitative investment will no longer be something mysterious and abstruse, even user has little knowledge about quantitative investment, he or she can adopt the advanced investment philosophy and technique by using our website.
3
THE PROCESS
After understanding the needs of users, I drew a flowchart of typical operations of a user using our website.
According to the typical operations of a user depicted in the flowchart above, we held several meetings to decide the website structure. Finally, we agreed that our website will consist mainly 5 parts, which are "Home" page that gives users a general idea of our website, "Strategy" page that functions as a platform of buying and selling strategy, "Generator" page with which users can generate their own strategy, "Help" page that provides guidance for new users and "My Account" pages on which users can manage and monitor their strategies.
After settling down the website structure, I began to work on prototypes. During the time, I had several discussions with Ling DONG, our front-end developer to decide the layout and animation of elements of our web page. Below are some of the prototypes that we made together.
Then, I set UI guideline in order to keep the overall style of our website consistent.
4
THE WEBSITE
(1) Log In and Sign Up
(2) Help
New users can get familiar with our website through this page, which includes answers to questions like "What do we do?", "Why choose us?", "What can I do with this website?" and "How to use this website?".
I used icons and screen shots to help illustrate. Moreover, I put magnifier around the important buttons so users can see the details clearly and get a direct feeling about how to operate the website.
(3) Generator
This page is designed for users to generate their own strategy, including input of strategy descriptions and parameters.
I used dashboard to present this web page so user can discriminate different sections easily. The challenging part of this web page is the section "High Level Index". In this section, users are supposed to choose index from index library and set parameters of the chosen index if there is any. Because the index library is multi-level structured, we thought of the file tree layout of windows system, so I divided this section into four parts, which are first level of menu of index, second level of menu of index, menu of chosen index and parameter form of chosen index. Moreover, when users choose an index, there will be an animation of an chosen index floating from the left to the right into the column of chosen-index menu.
(4) Strategy Detail
On this web page, user is able to see all the details of a strategy and also manage operations of a certain strategy such as "Add Back-test" and "Retrain". This web page includes a lots of elements like forms and charts and requires more interactions with the user.
I strengthened the important functions and numbers using different colors while keeping the color style consistent. Beyond that, in order to help user to gain a better interaction experience, I added animation on the curve graphs like "Yield Curve" and " Position Curve" and on the pie chart, the "Position". When users hover over a curve, the curve will obtain a shadow and that makes it looks like floating, and when users hover one part of the pie chart, a certain legend on the right will enlarge to help users to read the information corresponding to this part.
(5) My Account
By entering "My Account", users can view their own information and strategy details, also, users can manage and operate each strategy and modify settings such as text notices, account binding or password changing.
I adopted left navigation menu to cater for most of users' operation habits, as well as improve the efficiency of choosing menu items. This part includes lots of forms and the problem is all about how to present information and how to arrange buttons.

THE FUTURE AND SOME PERSONAL GAINS

Also, it was the first time that I work as designer in a formal team. I learned how to cooperate with other to build a product and how to communicate with colleagues when encounter problems. Anyway it was really a great experience working in GPX.
